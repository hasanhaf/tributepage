Satoru Iwata

One of the greatest gamers and industry leaders.

Here are some of achievments that Mr. Iwata has achieved.

  • Iwata was known for unconventional ideas and changing ideas and changing medium of gaming.
  • He was seen as an embodiment of Nintendo.
  • Iwata's proficiency in programming led many to refer to him as a "genius" in the subject.
  • His hands-on-approach to business earned him admiration from both developers and gamers.
  • By targeting a new audience instead of competing against Microsoft and Sony, Iwata avoided a "straight fight" with Nintendo's competitors and successfully achieved his goals.
  • Iwata would often forego media appearances, unless new hardware was being announced, in order to spend more time programming.
  • Iwata helped Nintendo to improve relations with its fans by regularly responding to them through social media,
  • Iwata helped to institute Nintendo Direct, a series of online press conferences open to all that revealed upcoming Nintendo games.
  • In June 2013, Iwata took on the additional role of Nintendo of America's CEO.
  • Iwata placed focus on development of products that improved quality of life.
  • Iwata oversaw development of the Nintendo Switch through the final months of his life, serving as the console's head developer.

"On my business card, I am a corporate president. In my mind, I am a game developer. but in my heart, I am a gamer."

Saturo Iwata 1959-2015
